Zhanliang Liu is a very technical engineering manager who is able to build complex system/products with experience in mature and startup companies, especially for search related area.

He is currently a principle enginner, senior manager of International Product Department at Baidu. Before joining baidu he was a principle engineer, manager of Sogou haomatong development group. Prior to Sogou, he was a researcher at Tencent working on Web Crawling, Information Integration, Information Retrieval and Data Mining. Before that, he was a research software development engineer in the Web Search and Mining Group at Microsoft Research Asia with research interests on Information Retrieval and Web Search Architecture. He holds six patents for his innovation in his research fields.

He also has a close relationship with the academic community. Currently he is the adjunct chief architect at Big Data Analytics & Intelligence Lab of Renmin University of China.

He was one of the founding team members of Hitchsters.com(Was named one of Time Magazine's 50 Top Websites for 2007. Successfully negotiated an all-cash sale to a competitor in 2010.)

He received his bachelor's degree and master's degree in Computer Science from Tianjin University, China, in 2005 and 2007, respectively.
